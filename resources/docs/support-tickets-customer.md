# How to Submit a Support Ticket

Use this guide when you need help with loans, repayments, profile, or account issues.

## Open the Support Page

- Page: [`/customer/support`](/customer/support)

The page shows your registered contact details and a support form.

## Submit a New Ticket

1. Enter a clear **Subject**.
2. Describe the issue in **Message** with enough detail to investigate.
3. Click **Submit Support Ticket**.

Your registered customer identity and contact details are attached automatically.

## Track Existing Tickets

On the same page, you can see recent tickets and statuses such as:

- `new`
- `in_progress`
- `resolved`
- `closed`

Use **View** on a ticket to read full details and any support response note.

## Tips for Faster Resolution

- Include loan number or repayment details when relevant.
- Mention date/time of failed transactions.
- Explain what you expected versus what happened.
