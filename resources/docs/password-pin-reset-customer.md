# Customer Password & PIN Reset

Use this guide for customer account recovery and PIN updates.

## Forgot PIN (Logged Out)

Use:

- [`/customer/password/forgot`](/customer/password/forgot)

Flow:

1. Enter your account identifier.
2. Complete OTP verification.
3. Complete security-question verification when prompted.
4. Set a new PIN and sign in again.

## Change PIN (Logged In)

Use:

- [`/customer/pin/change`](/customer/pin/change)

Recommended:

1. Choose a PIN that is not easy to guess.
2. Do not share your PIN with anyone.
