# How to Make Repayments

This guide covers the customer repayment flow.

## Where to Start

- Open dashboard: [`/customer/dashboard`](/customer/dashboard)
- Click **Make a Loan Repayment**

## Repayment Steps

### 1. Select repayment type

- Page: [`/customer/repayments/select-type`](/customer/repayments/select-type)
- Choose one option:
  - **Overdue Amount** (if available)
  - **Partial Payment**
  - **Full Payment**
- For partial payments, choose a specific loan (optional) and enter amount.

### 2. Select payment channel

- Page: [`/customer/repayments/select-channel`](/customer/repayments/select-channel)
- Choose your repayment channel.
- Enter phone number if needed for that channel.

### 3. Confirm repayment

- Page: [`/customer/repayments/confirm`](/customer/repayments/confirm)
- Verify repayment type, amount, channel, and phone number.
- Click **Confirm & Pay**.

### 4. Success and records

- Success page: [`/customer/repayments/success`](/customer/repayments/success)
- Check statement afterward:
  - [`/customer/statement`](/customer/statement)

## Important Notes

- Repayment amount is validated against your outstanding balance.
- If payment processing fails, retry from the confirmation flow.
- If issues continue, raise a support ticket: [`/customer/support`](/customer/support).
