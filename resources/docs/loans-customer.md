# How to Take a Loan

This guide explains the loan application flow for customer users.

## Where to Start

- Open your dashboard: [`/customer/dashboard`](/customer/dashboard)
- Click **Get a Loan** or **Apply for Another Loan**

## Standard Loan Flow

### 1. Select disbursement channel

- Page: [`/customer/loans/select-channel`](/customer/loans/select-channel)
- Choose where your loan should be sent (for example, mobile wallet or bank channel).

### 2. Enter loan amount and disbursement number

- Page: [`/customer/loans/enter-amount`](/customer/loans/enter-amount)
- Enter loan amount within your allowed limit.
- Confirm the disbursement phone/account number.

### 3. Review calculation

- Page: [`/customer/loans/calculate`](/customer/loans/calculate)
- Review:
  - principal amount
  - processing fee
  - interest
  - total amount to repay
  - monthly installment
- If prompted, select repayment tenure and recalculate.

### 4. Confirm application

- Click **Confirm & Apply** to submit the loan application.
- You will be returned to your dashboard where status updates appear.

## Collateral Loan Flow

Some products use the collateral flow instead of the standard flow.

### 1. Enter loan details

- Page: [`/customer/collateral-loans/loan-details`](/customer/collateral-loans/loan-details)
- Provide amount, tenure, start date, channel, and disbursement number.
- Click **Calculate**, then **Continue to Collateral**.

### 2. Provide collateral details and submit

- Page: [`/customer/collateral-loans/collateral`](/customer/collateral-loans/collateral)
- Complete collateral information and submit.

## After Submission

- Track active loans and balances on dashboard and statement pages:
  - [`/customer/dashboard`](/customer/dashboard)
  - [`/customer/statement`](/customer/statement)

If your application cannot proceed, submit a support ticket from [`/customer/support`](/customer/support).
